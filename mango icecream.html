Ingredients

  1. Heavy cream =1.5 cups
    2.
  2. milk1/2 cup(whole/skimmed)
  3. mango 1 no(medium size ripe)
  4. sugar1 cup

METHOD

To prepare mango puree, dice the pulp of fresh mango into chunks. Blend it to a smooth puree along with sugar without using any water. Or you can use cannned mango puree. Whip the cream using a hand mixer till soft peaks form. You can use a stand mixer as well for the purpose. Add mango puree in the whipped cream. Whisk using the hand blender or sptaula for 1 – 2 minutes or until well combined. Pour the ice cream into a freezer friendly container with a lid. Freeze for 5 – 6 hours or until set. Stand for 5 minutes to soften slightly, then scoop and serve!
